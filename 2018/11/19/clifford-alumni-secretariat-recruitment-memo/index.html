Clifford Alumni Secretariat Recruitment

Clifford Alumni (CA) is the only official alumni network endorsed by Clifford School (Guangzhou, China). Since last November, the Clifford Alumni Secretariat (CAS), along with members of CA, have successfully hosted numerous events for current students at Clifford. Recently, CAS is honoured to have invited Irene Lou for a keynote speech at this year’s senior high school graduation ceremony. We, CAS, are hoping to expand our projects with current students, as well as establishing a truly global network for Clifford’s own exceptional graduates over the teyears.

CA is rapidly developing, and we don’t feel like slowing down at all. Thus, we need creative, committed, and self-motivating talents to joining our team to transform our plans, and visions into reality. We hope that CA can be everlasting and will benefit all past, present, and future Clifforders.

We welcome applications from all over the world, because why not?

Permanent Positions

Overall requirement

Weekly Meeting and Contribution: Compulsory

Minimum Weekly Working Hours: 10.

Alumni Relations Officer

  1. Managing Alumni files, and operate alumni relations platforms;
  2. All Alumni-related issues;
  3. Coordinating with representatives from each alumnus year group.

Public Relations Officer

  1. Managing CA’s multimedia platforms, including but not limited to, Wechat, Facebook, and our website;
  2. Operating multimedia platforms, duties of which include but not limited to publishing articles, videos, and other advertising materials;
  3. Representing CA before the public.

Head of Graphics Design

  1. Designing posters, Powerpoint presentations, videos, and/or H5 pages for Wechat;
  2. In charge of your own team.

Event Officer

  1. Planing events such as, but by no means limited to, our Speakers’ Event,  and Alumni Homecoming;
  2. We’re open to new ideas.

Treasurer

  1. Financial reporting, planning, and budgeting;
  2. Funding, fundraising and sales.

Part-Time Positions Requirements

Weekly Meeting: At least twice every month, contribution optional.

Minimum Weekly Working Hours: 3.

  1. Graphic Designer;
  2. Content Contributor for social media platforms in Chinese and/or English;
  3. Event Planner
  4. Treasurer
  5. DIY

We welcome creative proposals for your own position.

To apply, please attach your Curriculum Vitae and/or your Resume to hr@cliffordalumni.com, we also appreciate a cover letter which briefly outlines why would you want to join us, and what can you bring to CAS Secretariat. We accept applications in Chinese and/or English.

祈福校友会筹委会成员招聘启事

祈福校友会是祈福英语实验学校唯一官方认可的校友会组织。自2016年11月以来，祈福校友会筹委会已多次在祈福英校成功主办了校友经验分享活动，给英校在校学生提供了与毕业校友交流的机会。今年6月，祈福校友会筹委很荣幸地邀请到优秀校友卢泳辰回校致辞，在祈福英校2017年高中毕业典礼上代表历届校友们为毕业生送上祝福。祈福校友会作为祈福学子与母校沟通的桥梁，旨在加强校友间、校友及母校间的交流与联系。校友会的成立与目前的发展离不开校方及校友们的支持与鼓励。为了扩大祈福校友会的规模、更好更快地建立祈福学子之间交流互助的平台，祈福校友会筹委会现诚邀有意支持与帮助校友会发展的校友们加入我们，让祈福校友会成为在外拼搏的祈福学子互助互利的加油站，成为英校人在奋斗之余能寻回年少初心的“家”。

我们欢迎世界上任何国家/地区的校友加入我们的团队, 只要你有心有才有能力, 我们就要你!

现诚聘有能力胜任以下职位的校友:

筹委会常驻成员

筹委会常驻成员需作为一个项目的主要负责人，每周至少有十小时服务校友会并参加周例会。

毕业校友联络员

  1. 管理校友资料收集平台（mailchimp，麦客，签到家）
  2. 参加例会并针对毕业校友资料收集提出方案及改进建议
  3. 管理/联络各年级毕业代表

媒体运营官

  1. 负责微信公众号/Facebook Homepage 的日常运营，如每周定时发送微信推文及更新Facebook主页
  2. 参加例会并汇报媒体运营情况，提出方案及改进建议

文案设计

设计活动宣传海报、PPT样式、宣传视频、H5等

中文文案编辑

  1. 负责审核编辑对外文案（微信推文，海报内容.etc）
  2. 撰写官方文件或需要呈递给领导的文件（建设计划，活动计划.etc）

活动策划

负责策划大型活动（Speakers’ Event，返校活动，线下同城.etc）

财务会计

  1. 管理校友会财务
  2. 寻找赞助商赞助校友会日常运营及活动费用

筹委会成员

筹委会成员需每月至少参加两次周例会。

每周工作时长: 3小时

  1. 文案设计

设计活动宣传海报、PPT样式、宣传视频、H5等

  1. 活动策划
    负责策划大型活动（Speakers’ Event，返校活动，线下同城.etc）
  2. 自由人 (我们欢迎有创意的人自行设计你的职位)

申请方式：请将您的履历或简历（中英文皆可）发送至hr@cliffordalumni.com完成申请。您也可以另附文件阐述以下问题，让我们更加了解你:

1. 为什么你想加入校友会筹委会

2. 你能为校友会筹委会带来什么？
